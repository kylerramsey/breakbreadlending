Next we talk Tradelines

Tradelines are the secret sauce for getting your best APR ever. Are you getting ready for a big purchase? New home? New car? Maybe youre getting ready for a large purchase for your business. Well tradelines are here to help you like you've never seen before! When you purchase a "Trade Line" you are becoming an active user on someone elses credit acount that is normally much larger than anything you have on your report. These trade lines also will have under 30% utilization so that it will not lower yours in any way. In fact, in most cases it will raise your overall credit, lowering your total usage and thus raising FICO. *The trick with these trade lines is that most other banks and card issuers will "piggy back" off of the high limits, or give you much better rates becasue of the outstanding credit report.
